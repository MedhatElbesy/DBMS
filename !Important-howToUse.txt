#To Use OS44-DBMS:

#1) At Your Home Directory
	ADD The 'Project' Directory To Your Home Directory

#2) Open Terminal And Follow These Commands:
	vi ~/.bashrc (In Your Home Directory)

#3) add This Line To '.bashrc' File:
	export PATH="$PATH:/home/'your username'/Project/"

#4) Restart Your Terminal

#5) Run This Command To Use OS44-DBMS
	dbms.sh
